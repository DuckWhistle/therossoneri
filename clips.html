Man Of The House trad.
recorded live, 6 June 2006 EXR Clip 1 mp3
Funky bass solo, building up through guitar and drums to a fast tune on violin and whistle.
Hailed as "best song in 21 years" of the open mic event, quite an achievement! Recorded at Googlies Singers' Night, Didsbury.
Star Of The County Down trad.
recorded live, 26 May 2006 EXR Clip 2 mp3
Rhythmic popular song supported by guitar, bass and bodhrán, with melodies and harmonies from two voices and violin. Recorded at Whit Acoustic Fest, 24rockfest.
