The Rossoneri

This is the website for the acoustic modern and folk band, The Rossoneri. We are based in Manchester, UK.

Next gig: "In The City" event, M19 bar, Levenshulme Manchester, Saturday 1st October 2005. We are playing in the Acoustic Showcase as part of the 24rockfest. Tickets £3, doors 7pm, contact Phil.

Music

We play violin, tin whistle, guitar and bodhrán, as well as vocals. We play traditional Irish tunes and songs, as well as covers of artists such as The Pogues, Van Morrison, Oasis and Sawdoctors, and other songs on request. A number of our own songs are available too, with lyrics written by Manchester poet Mike Duff.
