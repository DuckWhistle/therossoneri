This is the website for the acoustic modern and folk band, The Rossoneri, who were together between 2005 and 2007. They often played folk rock with a rhythm section called Extraordinary Rendition. They were based in Manchester, UK. Credit for the photo above, taken at the Carlton Club on 9 January 2007, goes to Ted.
